About Me

My name is Liam Lebenon, welcome to my website. I was born in Johannesburg, South Africa in the year 2000.

I graduated high school with a matriculation certificate in 2018 from Bryanston High School. I was never really sure what to do after school, I thought of learning a trade, going to college or even joining the army.

However, I have always had a great interest in computers, being big into gaming so I decided in 2021 to learn how to code. I started a Full Stack Developer course with ITCareerSwitch in March of 2021 and have been loving it ever since.

I have learned so much in the past few months and have seen my skills in HTML and CSS improve substantially. I am currently 30% through the Codecademy Full Stack Developer course. I am very excited to enter this enormous, challenging world that is coding and cannot wait to learn more about the tools of the trade.
